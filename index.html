José's Hose

Oh no! There's a fire in the city and José and Grant have to save the day.

Tutorial

This is a two player game. Grab a friend!

José extinguishes the fires, while Grant carries out the poor victims.

Use arrow keys to navigate José's hose and hold shift to have him kneel and keep his position. Grant is controlled with WASD keys and can throw the citizens with the spacebar.

Empty out as much of the building as you can before your time runs out, but be careful - if you hurt yourself, you will lose some time!

To reach some of the fires José can reposition his hose to a new fire extinguisher. Don't hesitate to throw the citizens out the window as Grant, they're very durable and don't mind the fall!
